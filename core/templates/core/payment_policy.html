Pricing and Currency

  • All prices are listed in Kenyan Shillings (KES) unless otherwise stated
  • USD pricing available for international students
  • Exchange rates are updated regularly and locked in at enrollment
  • Prices include all applicable taxes and fees
  • Corporate discounts available for group enrollments (5+ students)

Payment Schedule Options

Full Payment (Recommended)

  • Pay the complete course fee upfront
  • Immediate access to all course materials
  • 5% discount on total course fee
  • Priority support and resources

2-Installment Plan

  • 50% payment upon enrollment
  • Remaining 50% due after 30 days
  • Access to course materials after first payment
  • Small administrative fee may apply

3-Installment Plan

  • 33% payment upon enrollment
  • 33% due after 30 days
  • Final 34% due after 60 days
  • Available for courses over KES 50,000

Payment Deadlines

  • Full payment: Due immediately upon enrollment
  • Installments: Must be paid by the due date to maintain access
  • Late payments incur a 5% penalty after 7 days
  • Access may be suspended for payments overdue by 14+ days
  • Final deadline: 30 days after course start date

Refund Policy

Full Refund (100%)

  • Cancellation within 7 days of enrollment
  • Course cancellation by LUM Data Academy
  • Technical issues preventing course access (our fault)

Partial Refund (50%)

  • Cancellation 8-14 days after enrollment
  • Before accessing more than 25% of course content
  • Medical emergencies (documentation required)

No Refund

  • After 14 days from enrollment
  • After accessing more than 25% of content
  • Violation of terms and conditions
  • Completion certificates already issued

Invoices and Receipts

  • Digital receipts sent immediately upon payment confirmation
  • Tax-compliant invoices available for corporate clients
  • All transactions include unique reference numbers
  • Payment history accessible through student portal
  • Hard copies available upon request (additional fee may apply)

Corporate and Group Rates

  • Government/NGO: Additional 5% discount

Payment Issues

If you experience payment problems:

  • Contact our finance team immediately
  • Provide transaction reference numbers
  • Keep payment receipts as proof of transaction
  • Allow 24-48 hours for payment verification
  • Report M-Pesa issues to both LUM and Safaricom

Payment Security

Your payment security is our priority:

  • All online payments are encrypted using SSL technology
  • We do not store complete credit card information
  • Payment processors are PCI-DSS compliant
  • Regular security audits and monitoring
  • Fraud detection systems in place

Disputes and Resolution

For payment disputes:

  1. Contact our finance team within 30 days
  2. Provide detailed information and documentation
  3. We will investigate and respond within 7 business days
  4. If unresolved, escalate to management
  5. Final recourse through Kenyan consumer protection laws
