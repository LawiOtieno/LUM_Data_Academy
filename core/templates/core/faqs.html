Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our courses and services

To enroll in a course, browse our course catalog, select your desired course, and click "Enroll Now." You'll need to create an account if you don't have one, choose your payment method, and complete the payment process. You'll receive a confirmation email with access instructions within 24 hours of payment verification.

Prerequisites vary by course level:

  • Beginner courses: Basic computer skills and high school mathematics
  • Intermediate courses: Basic programming knowledge or completion of our beginner course
  • Advanced courses: 1-2 years programming experience and statistics knowledge
  • Masterclasses: Professional experience in related field

You can access course materials 24/7 through our learning management system. Most courses include weekly live Q&A sessions with instructors.

You have lifetime access to course materials once you complete payment. This includes all video lectures, assignments, projects, and resources. You can also access updated content and new modules added to the course at no extra cost.
